FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. You can invite your team and test with real data.

How quickly can we get started?

Most teams are up and running within 30 minutes. SPHAiR comes pre-loaded with industry-standard checklists, so you can start using it immediately without lengthy configuration.

Does it work offline?

Yes. SPHAiR is designed for real-world conditions. Your team can complete checklists and log work offline, and everything syncs automatically when connectivity is restored.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated so you only pay for what you use.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers. Enterprise customers can also arrange invoicing with net-30 terms.

Is my data secure?

Security is a top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Each organization's data is completely isolated, and we perform regular security audits. Enterprise customers can opt for on-premise deployment for full data control.

Can I customize the platform for my company?

Professional plans include company branding (your logo and colors). Enterprise plans offer full white-label customization and custom integrations with your existing systems.

What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include email support with a 24-hour response time. Professional plans get priority support, and Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager with 24/7 availability.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes. Annual billing saves you up to 20% compared to monthly billing. Use the toggle above to see yearly prices.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
